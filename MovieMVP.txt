MVP and possible Data tables for the Movie Web app

MVP
-Sign in
-Home pages
    -Displays a favorite list
    -displays a list of recently added movies
    -displays a list of wanted movies
-Favorites page
    -option to remove movies from favorites
    -option to add movies to favorites
-Add new movies page
    -api request for a movie information and form to manually add
    -option to add movie and info to your collection
-My collection page
    -display pages of the movies you already owned
    -option to remove movies
    -option to filter the collection
    -option to add movies and takes you to add movie page
-Wants page
    -displays your list of desired movies
    -button to add a movie directly to your collection that also removes from wants list
    -api search function
    -option to add searched movie to your wants list



Data tables
* User info table, user-id, user-name, username and password
* collections table, user-id, movie-name, movie-genre, movie-actors, movie-rating, and movie-release-date
* Favorites table, user-id, movie-name, movie-genre, movie-actors, movie-rating, and why its a favorite
* Wants list table, user-id, movie-name, movie-rating, and movie-genre
